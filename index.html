About Us

Hello! We are students apart of the Cybersecurity class at the Hillsdale Area Career Center. The main contributors of this project are administrators Sl66p and ColDog5044. The reason this project was created was to help our community solve issues with their systems easily with our online help desk, as well as helping students learn about fixing common technology, and this is crowdsourced by people just like you! If you have an issue with your device, please refer to the "Submit A Ticket" tab in the top left, or, click here.

About the Instructor

Mr. Wayne Boggs is the instructor for the cybersecurity class at the Hillsdale Area Career Center, as well as the overseer for the help desk you're using! He holds Cisco CCNA certifications and is a certified instructor in Intro to Networks, Routing & Switching, Cyber Ops and IT Essentials. Additionally, he's certified in multiple courses in Test Out, Microsoft Office, Microsoft Technology Associate, and CompTIA. If you have an issue that you believe a student may not be able to help you with, you may contact Mr. Boggs directly, via the contact tab in the top left, or, by clicking here.
